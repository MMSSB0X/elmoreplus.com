What is Elmore Plus?

Born from the chaotic halls of Elmore Junior High, Elmore Plus was built because standard social networks simply couldn't handle the weirdness of our town. Whether you are a blue cat, a walking balloon, or an 8-bit cloud, this is your home.

We believe in a completely unfiltered experience. No Bobert-controlled algorithms, no Joy virus infections, and definitely no limits on how many times Richard can post pictures of his sausages. It's fast, it's secure, and it's 100% Elmore.
